About Me

My name is Madeleine Arrhenius. I grew up in Orange County, California, and later moved to Arizona during high school. I’ve always loved the ocean and staying active, especially through soccer, sand volleyball, and football.

I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and returned in March 2025 from serving a 1.5-year mission in Fort Worth, Texas. During my mission, I learned Spanish and gained experiences that helped me grow in leadership, communication, and adaptability.

I chose marketing because it has interested me for as long as I can remember. I enjoy creativity, strategy, and learning how people connect with brands. As the oldest of five siblings, I’ve naturally developed a strong sense of responsibility and leadership.
